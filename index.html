Summary
I'm a 4th year computer science student at Carleton University, with honours in Game Development. I have done research at Carleton in the field of image processing. I have many sideprojects that I have worked on. Some topics I'm interested in include game development, graphics, and security.

I co-lead the Lame Jam, a game jam at Carleton University. I co-lead Hack All The Things, an Ottawa-based security competition. I have been an executive member of the Carleton Computer Science Society for four years.
